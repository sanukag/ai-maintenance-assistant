# BC-12 Packaging Belt Conveyor

## Safe isolation

Press the normal stop control, open the local isolator and apply a personal
lock. Verify that the belt cannot start from either control station before
entering the guarded area.

## Belt tracking

Run the empty conveyor at its lowest speed. Adjust only one tracking screw at a
time, using quarter turns. Allow the belt to complete three revolutions after
each adjustment. Never adjust belt tracking while a guard is removed.

## Photoelectric sensor

Code `S-14` indicates that the infeed photoelectric sensor remained blocked for
more than 20 seconds. Remove the obstruction and clean the sensor lens with a
dry, lint-free cloth. Confirm that the amber indicator changes state when a test
carton passes.

## Gearbox oil

Check the gearbox sight glass while the conveyor is stopped and level. The oil
line should sit between the two red marks. Use ISO VG 220 gear oil when topping
up; do not mix lubricant grades.
