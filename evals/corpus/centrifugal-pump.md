# CP-20 Centrifugal Process Pump

## Isolation

Stop the pump from the local control station. Isolate and lock out the motor,
close the suction valve, close the discharge valve and drain the casing. Confirm
zero pressure on the casing gauge before loosening any fastener.

## Mechanical seal inspection

A continuous leak greater than 10 drops per minute requires inspection. Confirm
that the pump is isolated and depressurised before removing the coupling guard.
Do not touch the seal faces with bare fingers.

## Bearing lubrication

Apply 12 grams of NLGI 2 lithium-complex grease to each bearing every 2,000
operating hours. Clean the grease nipple before connecting the grease gun. Do
not overfill the bearing housing.

## Low discharge pressure

If discharge pressure is low, check that the suction valve is fully open and
inspect the suction strainer for blockage. Check the direction of motor rotation
after electrical work. Do not run the pump dry while investigating the fault.
