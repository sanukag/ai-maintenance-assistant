# AC-40 Workshop Air Compressor

## Daily inspection

Before starting the compressor, inspect the receiver drain, pressure gauge and
air hoses. Do not operate the compressor when a hose is split or its coupling is
loose.

## Condensate drain

Stop the compressor and isolate its electrical supply. Allow the receiver to
cool, then vent the receiver until the pressure gauge reads zero. Open the drain
valve slowly and collect the condensate in an approved container. Close the
drain valve before restoring power.

## Drive-belt tension

Isolate and lock out the electrical supply before removing the belt guard. At
the centre of the longest span, the drive belt should deflect 10 millimetres
under moderate thumb pressure. Refit the guard before testing the compressor.

## High-temperature shutdown

If the compressor stops with code `T-07`, leave it isolated for 30 minutes.
Check that the ventilation grille is clear and that the cooling fan turns
freely. Do not repeatedly reset `T-07` without finding the cause.
